=== DUNGEON QUEST RPG - CONTROLS ===

EXPLORATION
-----------
W/A/S/D     Move
Z           Interact (stairs, treasure, boss)
X           Leave dungeon
Enter       Camp menu (use tent, view stats)
Tab         Inventory

BATTLE
------
1           Attack
2           Skill/Magic
3           Item
4           Defend
5           Flee (not vs bosses)

MAP SYMBOLS
-----------
@   You
#   Wall
.   Floor
>   Stairs down
<   Stairs up
$   Treasure
B   Boss
E   Entrance

TIPS
----
- Mix physical and magic classes
- Save MP for bosses
- Use tents to rest between floors
- Characters learn skills as they level
- Explore thoroughly for treasure
