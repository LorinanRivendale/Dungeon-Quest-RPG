# Dungeon Quest RPG - Playtest Build

## Quick Start
1. Run `rpg_game.exe`
2. Create a party of 4 characters
3. Choose a dungeon to explore
4. Fight monsters, level up, defeat bosses
5. Collect all 4 key items to unlock the final dungeon

## Job Classes

### Physical Classes
- **Knight**: Tank with high HP/Defense, learns power attacks
- **Black Belt**: High damage fighter, can self-heal with Meditation
- **Thief**: Fast attacker with utility skills

### Magic Classes
- **Mage**: Highest magic damage, 3 elements (Fire/Ice/Bolt)
- **Sage**: Balanced caster, both damage and healing
- **Priest**: Best healer, party-wide healing spells

## Controls

### Dungeon Exploration
- `W/A/S/D` - Move
- `Z` - Interact (stairs, treasure, boss)
- `X` - Leave dungeon
- `Enter` - Camp menu (rest with tent, view party)
- `Tab` - Inventory

### Battle
- `1` - Attack
- `2` - Skill/Magic
- `3` - Item
- `4` - Defend
- `5` - Flee (not available vs bosses)

## Map Legend
- `@` = Your position
- `#` = Wall
- `.` = Floor
- `>` = Stairs down
- `<` = Stairs up
- `$` = Treasure chest
- `B` = Boss room
- `E` = Entrance

## Tips
- **Explore adjacent tiles** - Map shows what's around you
- **Save MP for bosses** - Use physical attacks on weak enemies
- **Use Tents** - Rest between floors to recover HP/MP
- **Level up** - Grind in early dungeons if struggling
- **Learn skills** - Characters learn new abilities every few levels
- **Balance party** - Mix physical and magical classes

## Recommended Party
- Knight (tank)
- Priest (healer)
- Mage (magic damage)
- Black Belt or Thief (physical damage)

## Dungeon Order (by difficulty)
1. Cave of Earth (Easiest)
2. Water Temple
3. Sky Tower
4. Volcano Keep (Hardest)
5. Final Sanctum (Unlocks after collecting 4 crystals)
